About Fletcher

Hi, I’m Nathanael (but most folks just call me Nat). After over 11 years in the painting and decorating trade, I decided to take the leap and start my own business — and that’s how Fletcher’s StrokeWorks came to be.

I'm proud to offer a professional, reliable service for homeowners across Lancashire. Whether it’s a single room or a full house, I put the same care and attention into every job, aiming for fresh, clean, high-quality finishes.

I provide free quotes with no pressure — just honest advice and a clear idea of what you need and what it will take to get it done. I believe in fair pricing and leaving customers completely happy with the results and experience.

If you’re after someone you can trust to show up, get the job done, and take pride in their work, I’d love to hear from you.
